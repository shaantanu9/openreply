Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. What we collect

We collect your email address, account profile, Reddit/brand data you import, and basic device information needed for licence activation.

2. How we use it

We use your data to provide the OpenReply service, manage your licence, send account notifications, and improve the product.

3. Reddit credentials

OpenReply does not store your Reddit password or post on your behalf. All posting is manual from your own account.

4. Third parties

We use Supabase for auth and data storage. If you bring your own AI key, your API calls go directly to your chosen provider.

5. Contact

Questions? Email support@openreply.co.
