## Single long-form post (LinkedIn / Bluesky)

We just turned OpenReply from a market-listening engine into a full content-and-citation stack.

Here is what that means.

**Compose** now drafts posts, threads, articles, short-form scripts, YouTube scripts, follow-up replies, and repurposed posts — all in your agent’s voice, tuned for X, LinkedIn, or Reddit.

**Repurpose** lets you watch any account, click “Rewrite,” and instantly rewrite their post in your voice. Same insight, your framing.

**Auto-pilot** drafts one piece of content + one reply every day, so you start each morning with drafts to review instead of a blank page.

And then there is **GEO**.

Generative Engine Optimization is the discipline of getting cited by AI answer engines — ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini. OpenReply’s AI Visibility screen tracks your queries, classifies results as Cited / Competitor / Absent, and shows Share of Voice over time.

With a Perplexity API key, it calls Perplexity Sonar and matches the real cited URLs against your domain. No guesswork.

SEO optimizes for links. GEO optimizes for being named in the answer. OpenReply now does both.
