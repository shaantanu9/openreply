## Frequently Asked Questions
Everything you need to know about ReplyDaddy

### How does ReplyDaddy work?
+
ReplyDaddy uses AI to scan Reddit for posts matching your keywords and brand. It analyzes each opportunity using multi-factor scoring, then generates authentic, value-first responses that comply with subreddit rules. You review and manually post the responses - maintaining full control.
### Does ReplyDaddy automatically post to Reddit?
+
No, ReplyDaddy does NOT auto-post. We generate responses that you review and manually post. This keeps you in full control and ensures compliance with Reddit's terms of service. You decide what gets posted and when.
### Do I need to connect my Reddit account?
+
No, you never connect your Reddit account to ReplyDaddy. We use read-only access to scan public Reddit content. Your Reddit account stays completely separate and secure. You simply copy our generated responses and post them yourself.
### Is my Reddit account safe using ReplyDaddy?
+
Yes, absolutely. Since we never ask for your Reddit credentials or post on your behalf, there's zero risk to your account. We only read public Reddit data and generate responses for you to manually post. Your account security is never compromised.
### What makes ReplyDaddy different from Reddit automation tools?
+
Unlike automation tools that violate Reddit's terms, ReplyDaddy is a discovery and content generation assistant. We help you find opportunities and craft responses, but you maintain the human element by reviewing and posting manually. This keeps your engagement authentic and compliant.
### How accurate is the AI in finding relevant posts?
+
Our AI uses Claude Sonnet 4 to analyze posts across multiple dimensions with 70% weight on relevance. We scan hot, rising, and new posts to maximize coverage. The multi-factor scoring system ensures you only see truly valuable opportunities, reducing noise and saving time.
