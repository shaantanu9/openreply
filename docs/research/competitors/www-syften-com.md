## Frequently asked questions
###  What is Syften?
Syften is an AI-filtered keyword monitoring tool for the public web, Reddit, X/Twitter, Hacker News, forums, blogs, GitHub, YouTube, Slack communities, Bluesky, Mastodon, and other online communities.
It helps founders, marketers, and support teams find brand mentions, competitor mentions, problem phrases, and buying-intent conversations as they appear.
###  Is Syften a Reddit keyword monitoring tool?
Yes. Syften is a Reddit keyword monitoring tool that tracks Reddit posts and comments for chosen keywords, brand names, competitors, and problem phrases.
Reddit alerts can be delivered through email, Slack, RSS, API, or webhook, with a target Reddit delay of under 1 minute.
###  Do you really track Reddit live?
Yes, we aim for at most a **1-minute delay**. For live Reddit monitoring, Syften specializes in live content delivery instead of historical search, so it can run precise keyword filters and CPU-heavy matching logic that general search alerts usually cannot support.
###  How is Syften different from Google Alerts?
Syften replaces Google Alerts by monitoring the public web plus live discussion sources like Reddit, forums, Hacker News, GitHub, YouTube, podcasts, Slack communities, and X/Twitter.
It sends alerts by email, Slack, RSS, API, or webhook and supports precise filters plus AI filtering for noisy terms. Google Alerts can still be kept as a free backup, but it does not need to be your primary monitoring workflow.
###  How is Syften different from social listening tools?
Syften focuses on high-signal keyword alerts from the public web and community sources like Reddit, Hacker News, GitHub, forums, blogs, YouTube, and Slack communities.
Traditional social listening tools often emphasize dashboards, brand analytics, and large social networks. Syften is built for finding conversations you can act on quickly.
###  Is Syften a GummySearch alternative?
Yes. If you came here because GummySearch is shutting down, Syften can help cover some of the same monitoring workflows.
GummySearch currently lists Syften as a recommended alternative for cross-platform brand monitoring because Syften tracks keywords on the public web, Reddit, and 10+ other platforms.
###  How do you keep noise down while preserving signal?
AI filtering runs on top of your full mention stream and suppresses low-value traffic like spammy posts, repetitive posts, auto-promos, duplicates, and weak matches.
You keep control of your keyword filters, but your inbox stays focused on human conversations that are more likely to become sales, support, research, or marketing opportunities.
###  Can Syften find past brand mentions?
Yes, for archive-backed sources. Syften includes archive search, so you can look back and find recent community mentions from before you created an alert. This is useful when you want to see who has already talked about your product, company, or domain before you start monitoring new mentions.
During the trial, Syften immediately shows recent archive-backed mentions from the last 2 months.
Read more in the [archive documentation](https://syften.com/documentation#archive).
###  Can Syften monitor competitor mentions and buying-intent phrases?
Yes. Syften can monitor competitor names, product categories, pain points, problem phrases, comparison searches, and keywords that indicate buying intent.
This makes it useful for lead generation, customer research, competitor monitoring, founder-led sales, and finding relevant web, Reddit, or forum mentions before they go stale.
###  Can Syften help with competitor monitoring?
Yes. You can track a competitor's domain name, product name, founder name, and social accounts to see where they are mentioned and which threads they are active in.
For example, you can monitor a competing domain, their X/Twitter handle, GitHub username, Reddit username, or forum username. Syften will alert you when those names appear on monitored web pages and in monitored communities, so you can spot recommendation threads, support issues, comparison discussions, and places where competitors are engaging.
For the broader tool stack, see the guide to the [best competitor monitoring tools](https://syften.com/blog/competitor-monitoring-tools/).

No, unfortunately not.

###  Where can Syften send alerts?
Syften can send keyword alerts by email, Slack, RSS, API, and webhook. This lets teams route Reddit alerts, forum alerts, brand mentions, and competitor mentions into the workflow they already use.
###  Does Syften have an API?
Yes. Syften has an API for teams that want to pull web mentions, Reddit mentions, forum mentions, brand mentions, and competitor mentions into their own tools or reporting pipelines.
Read more in the [API documentation](https://syften.com/documentation#api).
###  Does Syften have a Slack integration?
Yes. Syften has a Slack integration, so keyword alerts from the public web, Reddit, forums, blogs, GitHub, YouTube, and other monitored sources can be delivered directly to a Slack channel.
###  Does Syften have AI integration?
Yes. Syften can use AI to filter noisy keyword matches and keep your alerts focused on relevant conversations.
Syften onboarding is fully automated. After you sign up, Syften researches your company, competitors, product names, and relevant conversation patterns, then creates the initial monitoring setup for you.
You do not need to write filters yourself to get started.
###  Does Syften support webhooks?
Yes. Syften supports webhooks, so keyword alerts can be pushed to your own application, internal workflow, CRM, enrichment system, or automation stack as soon as matching conversations are found.
Read more in the [webhook documentation](https://syften.com/documentation#api-webhooks).
###  Is Syften good for agencies?
Yes. Agencies can use Syften to monitor multiple customers from one account, with separate filters for each brand, product, competitor set, or campaign.
You can [tag filters](https://syften.com/documentation#tags) for different customers, then route matching mentions to the right Slack channels, API integrations, webhooks, or automated reporting workflows. This keeps client work separated and helps you catch relevant web mentions, Reddit threads, forum posts, social mentions, and community conversations quickly.
###  Who is Syften best for?
Syften is best for founders, marketers, indie hackers, support teams, and agencies that need to find relevant online conversations quickly.
Common use cases include web monitoring, Reddit monitoring, brand monitoring, competitor monitoring, lead generation, customer research, community monitoring, and support escalation.
###  Does Syften monitor X/Twitter?
Yes. Syften monitors X/Twitter keyword mentions with a delay of at most 15 minutes.
###  Does Syften monitor YouTube?
Yes. Syften can monitor YouTube search results for keyword mentions and send alerts when relevant videos are found.
