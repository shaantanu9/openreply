## Questions, answered.
How does the free trial work?
20 free credits on signup, no credit card needed. 1 credit is used each time LeadsRadar surfaces a relevant lead (relevance ≥45) to your feed. Scans that return zero leads cost nothing. Drafts are free and unlimited. When you've used your 20 credits, subscribe for $19/mo to get 200 credits per month.
What's included in the $19/mo plan?
Unlimited on-demand scans across Reddit and Hacker News, AI-drafted replies in 4 public tones plus a private DM for each lead, Blitz triage UI, and personalized search queries generated from your product context at onboarding.
How long does a scan take?
1 to 3 minutes. You hit "Run scan" when you have time, get a batch of fresh leads, and triage them in Blitz. Built for outreach at night, not real-time push notifications.
What sources do you monitor?
Reddit and Hacker News. More sources (Substack, Medium, Indie Hackers, Dev.to) are on the roadmap.
Does this only work if I'm a founder?
No. The job underneath is the same whether you sell SaaS, run an agency, coach 1-on-1, ship DTC products, or run a local service: find Reddit threads where someone is asking for what you sell, or complaining about what you'd fix, and reply. The founder narrative is the loudest example, but the mechanic works for any business whose buyers post on Reddit.
Do the AI drafts actually sound human?
We engineered the prompt to fight every common AI tell: no "definitely," no "hope this helps," no "I'd recommend," no LinkedIn voice. Drafts feel like a real person typing fast on mobile. Try the demo above.
Will this get me banned on Reddit?
LeadsRadar never posts for you. It surfaces threads and drafts replies you copy, edit, and send yourself. Drafts lead with a real answer and disclose you're the founder. Those two things keep mods and communities onside. Every word that goes out is yours.
How does it know which posts are relevant?
Two layers. First, embeddings rank every post by semantic similarity to your product description, which catches threads about your space even when they don't mention your exact keywords. Then GPT-4o-mini scores the top 100 from 0–100 and writes drafts for those above the threshold.
Will this flood me with customers?
No. Anyone promising that is selling you something. Reddit outreach is manual outbound with better timing: a handful of genuinely relevant replies a week, not 50 cold drops. LeadsRadar kills the 4-hour scroll and the blank-draft paralysis, not the part where you show up like a human. Done right, that handful converts far better than cold email.
