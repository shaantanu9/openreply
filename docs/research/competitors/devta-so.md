##  Frequently Asked Questions 
Will the AI Networking agent damage my professional reputation?
You have complete control over it. We designed the system keeping the "human in the loop" concept at the core, so you can view its activity live just like you’d watch your employee working. If it ever makes a mistake, you can stop and restart it. 
The agent is based on a Persona system - it asks for your name, background, and expertise so it can emulate your specific voice and technical depth. 
Based on your persona, it finds relevant posts in the right communities and comments on them in your voice - with real value, never promotional. From there, when you're ready, you can have it research those people into leads and then send a personalized DM grounded in the public exchange - each is a separate step you trigger yourself. 
This is **the end of cold outreach.**
Is it safe? Will the platform notice I'm using an agent?
This isn't like other robotic automations that happen in a dark background. Our agent mimics human behavior exactly - you can literally see it scrolling, moving the cursor around, clicking links, and typing letter-by-letter. Because it behaves like a human, it's very rare for a platform to flag it. 
We also include a safety layer of expensive residential proxies so it looks like you are signing in from your actual country. We still recommend keeping activity low on new accounts and moving slowly until you've built up a reputation. 
Can I really trust an agent to sound like an expert in my field?
Yes, because it doesn't just "chat" with generic responses. Through the Persona system, you share your unique professional background and technical knowledge. The agent uses that specific context to engage at a high level, ensuring it sounds like a seasoned professional rather than a bot. 
Why should I send a Devta "Plan" instead of a standard proposal or PDF?
Most proposals are just "pretty PDFs" that show off branding but fail to explain the execution. For developers, designers, and professional service providers, trust is won through logic.
A Devta Plan proves you actually understood the client's problem and have a systematic way to solve it. It wins trust where a generic proposal usually fails. 
What happens if the client has tricky technical questions or change requests days after I send the plan?
In many cases, the plan is so comprehensive that it covers everything upfront. However, if the client has new requests or technical questions later, your Strategic Assistant stays "attached" to that plan. 
It remembers all the original project context to help you provide fast, accurate answers without you having to dig through old notes. 
I have multiple prospects and complex calls - can I really handle all this alone?
Yes. You can hop on multiple calls with clients and simply upload the meeting recordings.
The assistant analyzes the transcript and even understands if the client was showing something visually on the screen, ensuring nothing is missed. This allows SDRs or solo founders to handle 10+ prospects at once with the precision of a full technical team. 
How does pricing work? Is there a subscription?
No subscription, no auto-renewals. You top up your account whenever you want - $49 minimum - and that becomes a single credit balance you can spend across every paid feature, in any mix. No tiers, no monthly fees, and your balance never expires. 
If you run out mid-task, the agent pauses cleanly - your account stays connected, your work is saved to session history, and nothing breaks. Top up again and pick up where you left off. We never auto-charge, so credits only get spent when you actively trigger an action.
